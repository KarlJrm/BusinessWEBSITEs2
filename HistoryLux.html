History

Lux boutique started as a humble little boutique shop at a local mall back in 2015, it was founded by our now CEO Ivaln Laguisma, many connections and collaborations were made along the way making the boutique a prominent clothes selling shop in the region, the business was thriving and able to sustain itself, But then disaster struck.

During the early 2020, covid 19 outbreak started and has affected many people, lockdowns were implemented due to the fear of contracting the virus. The boutique wasnâ€™t spared as well, the shop was entirely closed and halted all business, funds were dropping, and daily necessities were barely being provided by other businesses due to lockdowns, for this reason the delivery businesses thrived, an opportunity struck and the head of our business, he had the bold idea of implementing a delivery service of our products through online means.

At first we started by doing facebook livestreams selling and advertising our current products by using 3rd party delivery service and logistics to ship out our products to the customers, it was good while it was going but there was room for major improvements, the head had to gambled a major percentage of our earnings for a online shopping website and system, deliveries were handled by a 3rd party logistics company. It was rough at first, it took time to smooth out the problems but connections and collaborations with other brands has helped boost the popularity of the online shop, increasing our sales and further improving our business.

As of now, the boutique has grown bigger and has become a prominent online clothes store, very much respected and revered by many.
